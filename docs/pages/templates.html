Templates

ReqStudio ships with built-in templates for common SRS artifacts. Templates are available in Swedish and English and can be inserted into any document from the menu. Customize them as needed for your organization.

Available Templates

  • HL — High Level SRS
  • LL — Low Level SRS
  • CMP — Component specification
  • ADR — Architecture Decision Record
  • Ticket — Work item template with AC/DoR/DoD

Swedish Base Templates

Core sections include Sammanfattning, Scope, Aktörer, Krav, NFR, Risker, Arkitektur, Komponentkarta, flöden och test.

English Base Templates

Core sections include Summary, Scope, Actors, API/Data, NFRs, Risks, Architecture (C4), Component map, and Testing.

Usage Tips

  • Keep IDs, owners, version, and links (HL/LL/ADR) at the top of each doc.
  • Use ADRs to capture decisions and their consequences; link to SRS IDs and PRs.
  • Ticket templates help keep delivery aligned with SRS via AC and references.
  • Consider maintaining org-wide template variants in version control.
